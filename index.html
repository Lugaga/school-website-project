Scholar Life

Our unique approach to education will make you reimagine what is possible for your child. we brings a rigorous and innovative approach to education that focuses on developing each individual’s full potential.


Our students learn the material they need to excel at exams through methods that simultaneously develop the critical thinking, creativity, and strength of character that they will need to build successful and fulfilling careers in today’s world.


Our School is a Pan-African independent school network offering preschool through secondary education for students from ages 3 through 19. We prepare the next generation of leaders and innovators through world-class teaching methods with an emphasis on 21st century skills.

Clubs and Activities

Music

Our school is very active in music to nurture it as a talent, we support our students in every way we can.

Writing

The writing club is for students who have passion for writing essays, newspaper articles and blogs.
We encourage students to learn foreign languages and also do write ups in the same.

Dance

Traditional Dances is the main focus of our dance club, because we have students from different cultures and it is encouraged that we learn all of them.
